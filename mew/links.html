Low

L1 weights (link signals, client-side, instant):
Non-HTTPS: 30  ·  Display mismatch: 20  ·  Domain spoof: 10  ·  Hidden: 40  ·  High-risk TLD: 25
Shortener: 10  ·  Keyword: 15  ·  Brand exact: 5  ·  Brand fuzzy: 8  ·  No noopener: 15
Score = sum (no cap)

L2 weights (server headers, requires network call):
Not HTTPS: 4  ·  No CSP: 3  ·  No X-Frame-Options: 3  ·  CORS *: 3  ·  No HSTS: 2
No Permissions-Policy: 1  ·  No COEP: 1  ·  No COOP: 1  ·  No X-Content-Type: 1  ·  No Referrer-Policy: 1
Score = Math.min(20, sum)
